---

## Reply-handling rules

1. **First positive reply:** respond within 30 minutes during business hours, within 2 hours after-hours.
2. **Objection reply:** use `objection_library_ar.md` — don't improvise.
3. **Demo request:** send Calendly immediately → don't ask for preferred time; let them pick.
4. **"Send details":** send one-pager + demo CTA. Never send full deck unblock the demo.
5. **"Not now":** ask for specific follow-up date; if they can't commit, schedule +30 days.
6. **Silent after demo:** one gentle follow-up +2 days. If silent +5, move to long-term nurture.
7. **Every reply gets logged here — even "no thanks" replies.**

## Disqualified leads log

When a lead is firmly disqualified, record:
- Lead ID
- Company
- Disqualification reason (budget / timing / no fit / duplicate / competitor / no authority)
- Date
- Whether they're okay being re-contacted in 6+ months
