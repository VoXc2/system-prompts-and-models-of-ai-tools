# 📊 Dealix — The 3 Paying Customers/Day Model

**Target:** 3 paying customers daily = 90/month = ~1,080/year
**Status:** Not yet achievable (currently 0 customers)
**Honest baseline required:** First 10 customers establish conversion rates

---

## The Math (Conservative Assumptions)

```
Daily Paid = Leads × Reply × Demo × Show × Close × Pay

- Cold lead → reply:         5%
- Reply → demo booked:       40%
- Demo booked → demo show:   70%
- Demo → pilot/payment:      20%
- Pilot → paid:              80%

Composite: 5% × 40% × 70% × 20% × 80% = 0.224%

Required daily leads = 3 / 0.00224 = 1,340 cold leads/day
```

**This is unrealistic for solo founder-led outreach.**

---

## Realistic Paths to 3/day

### Path A — Improve Conversion (not volume)
Target warm/warmer leads to boost rates:
- Warm lead → reply:        30% (6x better)
- Reply → demo:             60%
- Demo → pilot:             35%
- Pilot → paid:             80%

Composite: 30% × 60% × 70% × 35% × 80% = 3.5% (15x better)

**Required: 86 warm leads/day** (still hard but possible)

### Path B — Partner/Agency Channel
Each agency partner = 10-20 clients pipeline:
- 10 agencies × 2 leads/week = 20 warm/week
- 3 agency partners = 60 warm/week = ~10/day
- With 3.5% conversion = 0.35 customers/day/agency partner

**Required: 10+ active agencies** to sustain 3/day

### Path C — Hybrid (Recommended)
| Source | Daily | Reply% | Demo% | Pilot% | Paid% | Customers |
|--------|-------|--------|-------|--------|-------|-----------|
| Warm outbound | 30 | 30% | 60% | 35% | 80% | 0.9 |
| Agency partner leads | 5 | 60% | 70% | 50% | 80% | 0.84 |
| Inbound (SEO/content) | 3 | 40% | 70% | 40% | 80% | 0.27 |
| Referrals | 2 | 70% | 80% | 60% | 90% | 0.60 |
| Paid ads (LinkedIn) | 10 | 15% | 50% | 30% | 80% | 0.18 |
| **Total daily** | **50** | | | | | **2.79/day** |

**Target: ~50 touches/day across 5 channels to hit 3/day**

## Stage Gates Before Hitting 3/day

### Stage 1: First 3 Paid (Week 1-2)
- Manual, founder-led
- Warm leads only
- Goal: Prove conversion funnel works
- **Required today:** 5 warm leads contacted

### Stage 2: First 10 Paid (Month 1)
- Start cold outreach
- First partner conversations
- Build initial case studies
- **Daily activity:** 20 outreach + 3 follow-ups

### Stage 3: First 30 Paid (Month 2-3)
- 3-5 agencies signed
- Content engine producing
- Paid ads $500-1000/month
- **Daily activity:** 40 touches

### Stage 4: 3/day Sustained (Month 4-6)
- 10+ agencies
- 5+ inbound leads/day
- Referral engine firing
- **Daily activity:** 50 touches across channels

## Honesty Check

**24-hour first customer = possible only with warm leads.**
**Cold outreach → first customer = 5-14 days typical.**
**3/day sustained = 60-90 days minimum.**

Anyone promising faster is lying or has pre-existing network.

Start with what you have:
1. Your WhatsApp contacts
2. Your LinkedIn connections  
3. One founder friend who will pilot as favor

That's your first customer today. Everything else is month 2+.
