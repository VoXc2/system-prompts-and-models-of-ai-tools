# Dealix Weekly Operating System (Saudi Enterprise Rhythm)

**Version**: 1.0  
**Status**: Active  
**Business Week**: Sunday to Thursday (Asia/Riyadh)

---

## Weekly Objectives

1. Keep critical workflows healthy (durable execution + SLA integrity).
2. Keep policy and approval surfaces current (no stale critical requests).
3. Keep executive visibility complete (forecast, risks, commitments, variances).
4. Keep release governance enforceable (no bypass, no missing attestations).

---

## Cadence by Day

## Sunday — Governance and Prioritization
- Review all `A2/A3` pending approvals.
- Validate policy violation board and unresolved exceptions.
- Publish weekly top 10 commitments by financial impact.
- Confirm board/executive memo queue for the week.

## Monday — Revenue and Partnership Execution
- Review funnel variance and next-best-action queue.
- Review partner scorecards and contribution margin shifts.
- Escalate any blocked strategic agreements (`R2/R3`).
- Confirm meeting and proposal orchestration SLAs.

## Tuesday — M&A / Expansion Execution
- Review target sourcing pipeline and DD room status.
- Review expansion canary metrics, stop-loss triggers, and readiness gates.
- Validate all term sheet and offer workflows carry evidence packs.

## Wednesday — PMI / PMO Discipline
- Review Day-1 and 30/60/90 commitments.
- Audit dependency bottlenecks and escalation aging.
- Review synergy realization deltas vs plan.

## Thursday — Executive and Release Control
- Finalize weekly executive room report:
  - actual vs forecast
  - risk heatmap
  - policy violations
  - committed decisions
- Run release gate checklist (rulesets, checks, deployment protection, provenance).
- Publish weekly trust and reliability summary.

---

## Weekly SLAs

| Signal | SLA | Escalation |
|-------|-----|------------|
| `A3` critical approvals | < 8 business hours | COO/CEO escalation |
| `A2` approvals | < 16 business hours | Function head escalation |
| Durable workflow stalled state | < 2 business hours to triage | Ops + domain owner |
| Policy violation unresolved | < 24 business hours | Security + domain owner |
| Connector degraded health | < 4 business hours | Integration owner |
| Evidence pack missing on sensitive flow | Immediate block | Trust plane hard fail |

---

## Weekly Output Pack (Required)

Each week must produce:

1. Executive weekly memo (Arabic + English summary).
2. Open approvals report (`A2/A3`) with aging.
3. Risk heatmap snapshot with top mitigations.
4. Durable workflow health report (running, blocked, retried, failed).
5. Release governance report (gates passed/failed and provenance status).

---

## Operating Commands (Implementation Intent)

- `Decision Plane`: schema adherence score and contradiction rate.
- `Execution Plane`: workflow completion and resume success rate.
- `Trust Plane`: policy denial ratio and approval latency.
- `Data Plane`: ingestion freshness and quality checkpoint pass rate.
- `Operating Plane`: release gate pass rate and environment protection compliance.
