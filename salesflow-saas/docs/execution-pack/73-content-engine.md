# Content Engine

## Content Pillars

### 1. Founder Authority
- Pain-point stories from real client scenarios
- "Here's what went wrong and how we fixed it"
- Industry-specific insights (healthcare, real estate, etc.)
- Platform: LinkedIn (primary), Twitter/X

### 2. Brand Education
- "How to" guides for B2B revenue operations
- Industry benchmark reports
- Product updates and feature announcements
- Platform: Blog, YouTube, newsletter

### 3. Objection Mining
- Convert every sales objection into content
- "We already use WhatsApp" → "WhatsApp without measurement is a leak"
- "CRM doesn't work for us" → "You don't need a CRM, you need a revenue OS"
- Platform: Social posts, blog articles, ad copy

### 4. Social Proof
- Case studies with measurable results
- Client testimonials (video preferred)
- Before/after metrics
- Platform: Website, sales decks, social

## AI Content Generation (via Content Agent)

### Supported Generation Types
- `generate_post()` — Social media post from topic/angle
- `generate_comment()` — Value-add comment for social post
- `generate_hooks()` — Hook variations for a given topic
- `generate_content_calendar()` — Monthly content plan
- `generate_hashtags()` — Relevant Arabic/English hashtags

### All generated content requires human review before publishing.
