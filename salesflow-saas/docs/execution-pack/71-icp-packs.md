# ICP Packs (Ideal Customer Profiles)

## Tier A Sectors

### 1. Clinics / Healthcare

**Profile**: Private clinics, dental centers, dermatology clinics, multi-specialty medical centers (5-50 staff)

**Revenue Leaks**:
- Patient inquiries via WhatsApp go unanswered for hours
- No follow-up after initial consultation inquiry
- No tracking of referral sources
- Appointments booked but no reminders sent → 30% no-show rate

**Buying Triggers**:
- Opened new branch/location
- Hired marketing manager
- Complained about "losing patients to competitors"
- Posted about being "too busy to follow up"

**Objections**:
- "We already use WhatsApp" → But you don't track or measure it
- "We don't need a CRM" → You need a revenue system, not a database
- "It's too expensive" → Calculate cost of 1 lost patient/month

**KPI Story**:
- Response time: from 4 hours → 15 minutes
- No-show rate: from 30% → 10%
- Patient conversion: from 20% → 40%
- Revenue per branch: +35% in 90 days

**Recommended Modules**: Lead capture, sequences (WhatsApp), appointments, SLA tracking

---

### 2. Real Estate

**Profile**: Real estate agencies, developers, property management companies (5-30 agents)

**Revenue Leaks**:
- Leads from Aqar/Haraj.sa not captured systematically
- Agents don't follow up after property viewings
- No attribution of which marketing channel generates deals
- Proposals (property offers) not tracked

**Buying Triggers**:
- Launching new project
- Hired new agents
- Advertising spend increased
- Complained about "leads not converting"

**KPI Story**:
- Lead response time: from 6 hours → 30 minutes
- Viewing-to-offer ratio: from 10% → 25%
- Agent productivity: 2x more deals/agent/month
- Marketing ROI visibility: from zero → full attribution

**Recommended Modules**: Lead capture, pipeline (custom stages), proposals, analytics, sequences

---

### 3. B2B Services

**Profile**: Consulting firms, IT services, accounting, legal, marketing agencies (3-20 people)

**Revenue Leaks**:
- Proposals sent but never followed up
- No pipeline visibility for partners/owners
- Client relationships dependent on individual memory
- No systematic referral tracking

**Buying Triggers**:
- Lost a major deal they should have won
- Partner wants to "professionalize sales"
- Growing team, losing control of pipeline

**KPI Story**:
- Pipeline visibility: from zero → real-time dashboard
- Proposal follow-up: from manual → automated
- Win rate: from 15% → 30%
- Avg sales cycle: reduced by 25%

**Recommended Modules**: Pipeline, proposals, contracts, analytics, follow-up sequences

---

### 4. Education / Training

**Profile**: Training institutes, language schools, private academies, online course providers

**Revenue Leaks**:
- Inquiry forms submitted but no follow-up
- Course registrations abandoned
- No nurture sequence for interested-but-not-ready students
- Events/webinars generate leads but no conversion tracking

**KPI Story**:
- Inquiry-to-enrollment: from 8% → 20%
- Follow-up within 1 hour: from 15% → 90%
- Course fill rate: from 60% → 85%

**Recommended Modules**: Lead capture, booking (for info sessions), sequences, analytics

---

### 5. Industrial Suppliers / Contractors

**Profile**: Building materials, equipment suppliers, maintenance contractors, wholesalers

**Revenue Leaks**:
- RFQ (Request for Quote) responses delayed → lost to competitor
- No tracking of repeat purchase patterns
- Sales team relies on personal relationships, no system
- No visibility into which projects generate the most revenue

**KPI Story**:
- RFQ response time: from 2 days → 4 hours
- Quote-to-order conversion: from 20% → 40%
- Customer reorder rate: +50%
- Revenue per rep: +30%

**Recommended Modules**: Lead management, proposals (quotes), customer management, analytics, SLA tracking
