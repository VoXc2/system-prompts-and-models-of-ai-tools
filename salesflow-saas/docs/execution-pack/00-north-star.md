# Dealix North Star

## Company Vision
Build the strongest B2B Revenue Infrastructure Company in Saudi Arabia, then expand globally.

## Company Mission
Eliminate revenue leakage for B2B companies by replacing fragmented sales tools with a single, AI-governed Revenue Operating System that captures, qualifies, routes, follows up, closes, and measures every revenue opportunity.

## Product Mission
Deliver an operator-grade, multi-tenant Revenue OS that works as:
1. **SaaS product** — self-serve signup, instant value
2. **Managed revenue operating system** — Dealix team operates the engine for the client
3. **White-label partner platform** — agencies/consultants resell under their brand
4. **Deployable revenue layer** — API-first integration into client infrastructure
5. **Internal operator console** — Dealix team manages all client instances

## North Star Metric
**Pipeline Velocity (SAR/day)** = (Qualified Opportunities x Avg Deal Size x Win Rate) / Avg Sales Cycle Days

## Non-Negotiables
- Multi-tenant from day one
- Arabic-first, English-ready (RTL native)
- Product mode + managed-service mode
- Integration-first (embeddable, API-driven)
- White-label capable
- Operator-grade dashboards
- AI-assisted but governed (human approval on public actions)
- No fake identities or prohibited automation
- Consent, opt-out, suppression, do-not-contact enforcement
- PDPL (Saudi Data Protection Law) compliance by design
