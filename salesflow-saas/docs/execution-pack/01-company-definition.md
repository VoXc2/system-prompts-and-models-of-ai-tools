# Company Definition

## Identity
**Dealix** — AI Revenue Operating System for B2B Companies

## What We Are
A B2B Revenue Infrastructure Company. Not a CRM company. Not a marketing agency. Not an AI tool.

We build, operate, and sell revenue infrastructure that eliminates the gap between "lead captured" and "deal closed."

## 5 Business Units in 1 Company

### 1. Demand Generation
- Content engine (founder authority, brand authority, objection mining)
- Social listening + human-approved engagement
- Inbound capture (forms, widgets, booking)
- ABM targeting

### 2. Sales Development
- Lead qualification (AI-scored, human-verified)
- Routing and assignment
- Sequence execution (WhatsApp, email, SMS)
- Follow-up discipline enforcement

### 3. Revenue Operations
- Pipeline management and velocity tracking
- SLA monitoring and breach alerting
- Attribution and channel performance
- Executive reporting and forecasting

### 4. Closing Enablement
- Proposal generation and tracking
- Contract management and e-signature
- Meeting preparation and follow-up
- Reactivation of stalled deals

### 5. AI Infrastructure for Revenue
- 20+ governed AI agents
- Structured output contracts
- Eval-driven quality improvement
- Cost tracking and provider management
- Human-in-the-loop approval gates

## Customer Types
1. **Self-serve SaaS** — SMEs who sign up and configure themselves
2. **Managed service** — Companies who pay Dealix to operate their revenue engine
3. **White-label partners** — Agencies/consultants who resell Dealix under their brand
4. **Enterprise** — Large organizations with custom deployment needs

## Commercial Principles
- Land with setup, expand with managed service, upsell with performance layer
- Time-to-value under 7 days for self-serve, under 14 days for managed
- Every interaction must be measurable and attributable
- Never charge for features that should be table stakes (audit logs, basic reporting)
- Price on value delivered, not seats occupied
