# GTM War Machine

## Positioning
**Category**: B2B Revenue Infrastructure
**Tagline**: "نظام تشغيل الإيرادات" (Revenue Operating System)
**Not**: CRM, marketing tool, AI chatbot, sales automation

## Category Narrative
> Most Saudi B2B companies lose 40-60% of their revenue opportunities. Not because they lack leads, but because they lack infrastructure. Leads come in, nobody follows up. Meetings happen, nothing gets documented. Proposals go out, nobody tracks them. Dealix is not another tool — it's the operating system that makes revenue execution systematic, measurable, and AI-assisted.

## Demand Generation Channels

### Owned (Content Engine)
1. **Founder authority content** — LinkedIn, Twitter/X (Arabic + English)
2. **Brand educational content** — Blog, YouTube shorts, podcast
3. **Webinars** — Monthly sector-focused (clinics, real estate, etc.)
4. **Lead magnets** — ROI calculator, industry benchmarks, playbook previews

### Earned (Social Listening)
1. **Topic monitoring** — Track keywords related to B2B sales challenges
2. **Competitor monitoring** — Track mentions of competitors
3. **Comment engagement** — AI-drafted, human-approved value-add comments
4. **Warm lead creation** — Convert engaged contacts to leads

### Paid (Targeted)
1. **Google Ads** — Industry-specific keywords (عيادات CRM, نظام مبيعات)
2. **LinkedIn Ads** — Decision-maker targeting by industry
3. **Meta Ads** — Retargeting and lookalike audiences

### Direct (ABM)
1. **Account targeting** — Build lists of target companies per sector
2. **Stakeholder mapping** — Identify decision-makers
3. **Personalized outreach** — WhatsApp + email sequences
4. **Signal-based prioritization** — Engage when buying signals detected
