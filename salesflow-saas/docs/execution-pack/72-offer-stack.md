# Offer Stack

## 7 Sellable Products

### 1. Revenue Engine Setup (One-Time)
**Price**: 15,000 - 50,000 SAR
**Delivery**: 7-14 days
**Includes**:
- Platform configuration for industry
- Pipeline stages setup
- WhatsApp Business API connection
- 5 sequence templates (industry-specific)
- 3 form/widget templates
- Team training (2 hours)
- Data import (up to 5,000 leads)
- 30-day post-setup support

### 2. Managed Pipeline Growth (Monthly)
**Price**: 10,000 - 25,000 SAR/month
**Includes**:
- Dealix team operates lead qualification
- Sequence execution and optimization
- Weekly pipeline review calls
- Monthly performance reports
- SLA-governed response times
- AI agent management
- Content recommendations

### 3. AI Sales Operating System (SaaS)
**Price**: 1,500 - 5,000 SAR/month (by tier)
**Includes**:
- Full platform access
- AI agents (by plan tier)
- Sequences and automation
- Analytics and reporting
- Self-serve management

### 4. Outbound & Reactivation Program (Project)
**Price**: 8,000 - 20,000 SAR/month (3-month minimum)
**Includes**:
- Target account list building
- Outbound sequence design and execution
- Dormant lead reactivation
- WhatsApp + email outreach
- Meeting booking
- Weekly reports

### 5. Revenue Recovery Sprint (One-Time)
**Price**: 10,000 - 30,000 SAR
**Delivery**: 30 days
**Includes**:
- Audit existing pipeline and processes
- Identify top revenue leaks
- Implement quick fixes (sequences, SLAs, routing)
- Reactivate dormant pipeline
- Deliver ROI report

### 6. Executive Reporting Layer (Add-On)
**Price**: 3,000 - 5,000 SAR/month
**Includes**:
- CEO dashboard with velocity + forecast
- Weekly automated executive summary
- Channel attribution reporting
- Custom KPI tracking
- Monthly strategy call

### 7. White-Label Partner Revenue OS
**Price**: Custom (revenue share or license)
**Includes**:
- Full white-label branding
- Custom domain
- Partner dashboard (multi-client)
- Sub-tenant management
- Co-branded marketing materials
- Partner enablement program

## Revenue Model Summary

| Layer | Type | Example |
|-------|------|---------|
| Setup | One-time | 15,000-50,000 SAR |
| Platform | Monthly recurring | 1,500-25,000 SAR/mo |
| Performance | Variable | 5-15% of attributed revenue |

## Pricing Philosophy
- Anchor on value delivered, not features
- Every tier should have clear ROI story
- Managed service commands premium because of outcome guarantee
- Performance layer aligns incentives (we win when you win)
