# Sector Playbooks

## Playbook Structure (per sector)

Each sector playbook defines:
1. Target persona and pain points
2. Pipeline stages (custom per industry)
3. Outreach sequence templates
4. Qualification criteria
5. KPI targets
6. Content angles
7. Onboarding assumptions

---

## Playbook 1: Clinics / Healthcare

### Target Persona
- **Decision Maker**: Clinic owner / Medical Director
- **Influencer**: Operations Manager / Marketing Manager
- **User**: Reception team / Patient coordinators

### Custom Pipeline
```
inquiry → consultation_booked → consultation_done → treatment_proposed → treatment_started → completed
```

### Outreach Sequence (WhatsApp)
```
Step 1 (0h):  "مرحباً {name}! شكراً لتواصلك مع {clinic}. كيف نقدر نساعدك؟"
Step 2 (4h):  "هل تحب نحجز لك موعد استشارة مجانية مع الدكتور؟"
Step 3 (24h): "تذكير: موعدك متاح. نقدر نخدمك في أقرب وقت يناسبك"
Step 4 (72h): AI-personalized based on inquiry type
Step 5 (7d):  "نتمنى لك الصحة والعافية. إذا تحتاج أي استشارة مستقبلاً..."
```

### Qualification Criteria
- Budget: Treatment cost > 1,000 SAR
- Urgency: Needs treatment within 30 days
- Insurance: Has insurance coverage (bonus)
- Location: Within clinic service area

### KPI Targets
- First response: < 15 minutes
- Inquiry-to-appointment: 40%
- Appointment no-show: < 15%
- Treatment conversion: 60%

## Playbook 2: Real Estate

### Custom Pipeline
```
inquiry → viewing_scheduled → viewing_done → offer_made → negotiation → deal_closed
```

### Outreach Sequence
```
Step 1 (0h):  "مرحباً {name}! شكراً لاهتمامك بـ{property}. متى يناسبك المعاينة؟"
Step 2 (2h):  Share property details + images
Step 3 (24h): "هل عندك أي أسئلة عن العقار؟"
Step 4 (72h): Share similar properties if no response
Step 5 (7d):  Market update + new listings
```

### KPI Targets
- First response: < 30 minutes
- Inquiry-to-viewing: 35%
- Viewing-to-offer: 25%
- Offer-to-close: 50%

## Playbook 3: B2B Services

### Custom Pipeline
```
discovery → needs_analysis → proposal → negotiation → contract → closed_won
```

### Outreach Sequence
```
Step 1 (0h):  "مرحباً {name}! شكراً لتواصلك. خلنا نحدد موعد لمناقشة احتياجاتك"
Step 2 (24h): Share relevant case study
Step 3 (72h): "هل الموعد المقترح يناسبك؟"
Step 4 (7d):  Share industry insight/article
Step 5 (14d): "هل لا زلت تبحث عن حل لـ{pain_point}؟"
```

### KPI Targets
- First response: < 1 hour
- Lead-to-meeting: 30%
- Meeting-to-proposal: 50%
- Proposal-to-close: 35%
- Avg sales cycle: 21 days
